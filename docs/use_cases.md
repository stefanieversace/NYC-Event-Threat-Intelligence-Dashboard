# 🎯 Use Cases

This system is designed to simulate real-world security intelligence workflows for high-density urban environments, particularly in New York City.

---

## 🎵 Protecting a Concert at Madison Square Garden

**Scenario:**  
A sold-out concert is scheduled at Madison Square Garden with high attendee volume and media presence.

**How the system supports operations:**
- Monitors OSINT feeds for crowd-related risks (e.g. congestion, disturbances)
- Detects nearby incidents such as suspicious packages or emergency response activity
- Flags high-risk alerts impacting venue access and perimeter security
- Provides real-time map visualisation of nearby threats
- Generates an analyst briefing for security teams prior to event start

**Operational Value:**
- Improves situational awareness for venue security  
- Supports proactive crowd management decisions  
- Enables rapid escalation of high-risk incidents  

---

## 🪧 Monitoring Protests Near Times Square

**Scenario:**  
A public demonstration is announced near Times Square, a high-traffic area with ongoing events and tourist activity.

**How the system supports operations:**
- Identifies protest-related keywords from news and social feeds  
- Maps protest locations relative to venues and event routes  
- Classifies disruption risk (e.g. road closures, crowd spillover)  
- Tracks escalation indicators such as police activity or unrest  
- Highlights affected assets (people, transport, venue access)

**Operational Value:**
- Helps security teams anticipate crowd movement  
- Supports planning for access control and rerouting  
- Enhances coordination with public safety agencies  

---

## 🚇 Assessing Transport Disruption Impact

**Scenario:**  
Subway delays and transport disruptions occur ahead of a major event.

**How the system supports operations:**
- Detects transit-related incidents (delays, outages, closures)  
- Links disruptions to nearby venues and event schedules  
- Assesses impact on attendee ingress and egress  
- Prioritises alerts affecting high-density areas  
- Includes transport risks in analyst briefings  

**Operational Value:**
- Improves planning for attendee arrival patterns  
- Reduces congestion risks at entry points  
- Enables contingency planning for alternative routes
